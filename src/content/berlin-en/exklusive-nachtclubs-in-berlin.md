Berlin is famous for its stripped-back, come-as-you-are club culture - but it also has a glamorous side. If you want table service, a dressed-up crowd and a bit of sparkle, the city's exclusive clubs and lounges deliver a very different kind of night from the warehouse floors.

## Key takeaways

- Berlin has an upscale nightlife scene alongside its underground clubs.
- Expect guest lists, table service and a dressed-up crowd.
- Reservations and advance planning make a real difference.
- A polished alternative to the techno temples.
- Concentrated in the west and central neighborhoods.

## What to expect

These venues flip the usual Berlin script: dress to impress, book a table in advance, and expect bottle service and a more curated door. The crowd skews glamorous and the drinks are premium. It's the night out for a special occasion rather than an all-weekend marathon.

## Getting in

For the best rooms, reserve ahead and confirm the dress code - trainers and casual gear often won't cut it here. Weekend nights book up, so plan early.

## Plan your night

The upscale scene leans toward [Charlottenburg](/en/berlin/nachtclubs-in-charlottenburg/) and [Mitte](/en/berlin/nachtclubs-in-mitte/). Pair it with a [stylish hotel nearby](/en/hotels/) to keep the glamour going.
