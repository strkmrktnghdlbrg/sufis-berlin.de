For all its techno fame, Berlin knows how to throw a hip-hop party. From old-school and boom-bap nights to trap, R&B and Afrobeats, the city has clubs and recurring events where the bass is heavy, the crowd is up for it, and the vibe is pure celebration.

## Key takeaways

- Berlin has a strong hip-hop, trap and Afrobeats scene.
- Look for recurring nights as much as fixed venues.
- Expect high energy, big sing-alongs and a party crowd.
- A great alternative to the techno floors.
- Central and eastern neighborhoods host most of the action.

## Clubs and nights

Much of Berlin's hip-hop scene runs on themed nights that move between venues, so it pays to check listings before you go out. Clubs like Prince Charles and various rooms across Kreuzberg and Mitte regularly host hip-hop, R&B and Afrobeats events that pack the floor. Bigger touring acts play the city's concert venues year-round.

## Getting in

The door is generally friendlier than at the techno temples, but popular nights fill fast - arrive earlier than you would for a warehouse rave, and buy advance tickets for headline events.

## Plan your night

Most nights land in [Kreuzberg](/en/berlin/nachtclubs-in-kreuzberg/) and [Mitte](/en/berlin/nachtclubs-in-mitte/). Grab a [hotel nearby](/en/hotels/) so you can dance late and stroll home.
