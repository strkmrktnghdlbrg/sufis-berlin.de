One of the best things about Berlin nightlife is that you don't need deep pockets to enjoy it. The city is famously affordable, with low cover charges, cheap drinks and plenty of free or student-priced nights. For younger travelers and students, it's one of the great value party capitals of Europe.

## Key takeaways

- Berlin is one of Europe's most affordable nightlife cities.
- Look for student nights, free entry and low cover charges.
- Bars and clubs in the east and Neukölln are the best value.
- Pre-drinking at a späti (corner shop) is a local tradition.
- Great music doesn't have to be expensive here.

## Where to go on a budget

Neukölln's bar scene is a budget dream - drift from one spot to the next without paying a cover. Many clubs run cheaper early-entry deals or student nights midweek, and the späti (Berlin's late-night corner shops) let you grab an affordable drink for the walk between venues. Even the big clubs are reasonable by international standards.

## Stretching your money

Go out earlier for lower cover, buy drinks at spätis between bars, and check listings for free parties. Berlin proves you can have an unforgettable night without spending a fortune.
