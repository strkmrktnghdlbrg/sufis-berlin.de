Kreuzberg is Berlin's counterculture heart, and its nightlife runs deep and wide. This is a neighborhood of canal-side bars, legendary music venues and all-night parties, where the crowd is diverse, the vibe is unpretentious, and there's always somewhere still open. Whatever your scene, Kreuzberg has a room for it.

## Key takeaways

- Kreuzberg is one of Berlin's richest nightlife neighborhoods.
- Strong in live music, bars and eclectic club nights.
- The mood is diverse, creative and come-as-you-are.
- Walkable, with the Oberbaum Bridge to Friedrichshain.
- Great for a bar crawl or a full night out.

## The scene

Institutions like SO36 and Lido have hosted live music for decades, while riverside and canal-side bars keep the drinks flowing late. Club nights here span everything from techno to hip-hop and everything experimental in between. Because so much is within walking distance, Kreuzberg is made for wandering from one spot to the next.

## An easy launchpad

The Oberbaum Bridge connects Kreuzberg to Friedrichshain's techno floors, so you can base yourself here and reach two of the city's best nightlife neighborhoods on foot.
