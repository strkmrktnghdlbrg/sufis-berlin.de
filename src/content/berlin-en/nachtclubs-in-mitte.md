Mitte is where Berlin's history and nightlife meet. The central district puts the city's landmarks, cocktail bars and clubs within easy reach of one another, making it a natural base for visitors who want to sightsee by day and go out by night without long journeys in between.

## Key takeaways

- Mitte is Berlin's central district, ideal for combining sights and nightlife.
- Home to the historic Tresor, plus bars and cocktail lounges.
- Great for cocktails, upscale nights and a central base.
- Everything is close - landmarks, bars and transit.
- The most convenient neighborhood for first-time visitors.

## The scene

Tresor, one of the founding clubs of Berlin techno, still pulls dancers into its tunnel floor. Around it, Mitte offers a polished mix of cocktail bars, hidden speakeasies and lounges, with the occasional club night thrown in. It's a more central, dressed-up flavor of the city's nightlife, easy to combine with a day of sightseeing.

## Perfectly placed

From Mitte you can walk to the Brandenburg Gate and Museum Island by day and be a short ride from the eastern club districts by night - the best of both worlds.
