Neukölln is where young, creative Berlin goes out. This is bar country - dive bars and cocktail spots packed shoulder to shoulder, rooftop terraces, natural-wine rooms and late-night falafel to soak it all up. Less about big clubs, more about drifting from one great little venue to the next, it's the perfect neighborhood for an easygoing crawl.

## Key takeaways

- Neukölln has Berlin's most vibrant, creative bar scene.
- More about bars and small venues than warehouse clubs.
- Rooftops like Klunkerkranich are local institutions.
- The crowd is young, international and up for it.
- Excellent value and highly walkable.

## The scene

The streets around Weserstrasse and the Reuterkiez are wall-to-wall bars, from grungy dives to inventive cocktail rooms. Klunkerkranich, a rooftop bar above a parking garage, is a must for sunset drinks with a skyline view. Small clubs and party spaces round out the mix, and the whole neighborhood is best explored on foot.

## An easy night out

Neukölln is made for wandering - no cover charges, cheap drinks and somewhere new around every corner. It's one of the best-value nights in the city.
