Tempelhof's nightlife is defined by open space. The former airport - now the vast Tempelhofer Feld - and the neighborhoods around it come alive in the warmer months with open-air parties, festivals and events on a scale few cities can match. It's a summer-first scene, backed up by quick links to Kreuzberg's clubs year-round.

## Key takeaways

- Tempelhof shines in summer with open-air events.
- The former airfield hosts festivals and large-scale parties.
- More seasonal than the round-the-clock club districts.
- Quick links to Kreuzberg's bars and clubs.
- A calm, spacious base with the city close by.

## The scene

When the weather warms up, Tempelhofer Feld and its surroundings host open-air gatherings, from music events to festivals, that make the most of all that space and sky. It's a different flavor of Berlin nightlife - outdoors, communal and sun-soaked. Off-season, the neighborhood is quieter, but Kreuzberg's floors are only a short ride north.

## Timing your visit

For the full Tempelhof experience, come between late spring and early fall and keep an eye on the events calendar. Our [open-air guide](/en/berlin/open-air-nachtclubs/) tracks the best of it.
