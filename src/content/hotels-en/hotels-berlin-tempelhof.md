Tempelhof is defined by its extraordinary centerpiece: the former airport, now a vast public park where Berliners cycle, skate and picnic on old runways. The neighborhood around it is comfortable, green and easygoing, with hotels and apartments that offer solid value and space to breathe. It's a relaxed base with the openness of Tempelhofer Feld on your doorstep and quick links to the rest of the city.

## Key takeaways

- Tempelhof centers on the former airport, now one of Europe's great urban parks.
- Hotels and apartments here are comfortable and good value.
- Family-friendly and pet-friendly options are easy to find.
- A calm, spacious base with strong transit links.
- Great for open-air events and warm-weather nightlife.

## Wide-open space in the city

Tempelhofer Feld is unlike anything else in Berlin - miles of open runway and meadow where you can bike, jog or just watch the sky. The surrounding streets are residential and unhurried, and the hotels tend to be practical and comfortable, often with room to spread out. It's an ideal choice if you want a bit of calm without leaving the city.

## Open-air nights

In the warmer months, the airfield and its edges host open-air parties and events, giving Tempelhof a nightlife of its own. Our guides to [open-air clubs](/en/berlin/open-air-nachtclubs/) and [nightlife in Tempelhof](/en/berlin/nachtclubs-in-tempelhof/) point you toward what's on, while the U-Bahn carries you to the indoor clubs when you want them.

## Comfortable and well linked

Tempelhof's location just south of Kreuzberg means the bars and clubs of the inner city are close, and its transit links make the whole city accessible. Combine that with the value on offer, and it's a smart, comfortable base.

## Frequently asked questions

### What's special about Tempelhof?

The former airport, now Tempelhofer Feld - a huge public park that's become a Berlin icon for cycling, skating and open-air events.

### What are the hotels like?

Comfortable and good value, with plenty of family-friendly and apartment-style options.

### Is there nightlife nearby?

Yes, especially open-air events in summer, plus quick links to Kreuzberg and the inner-city clubs.

### Is it a good base for families?

Very. The park and the space-friendly hotels make it an easy choice for families.
